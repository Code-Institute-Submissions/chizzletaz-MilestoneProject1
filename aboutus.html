About Be-Sharp

The Dutch vocal group Be Sharp is group of 4 singers, 2 men and 2 women, that was founded in 2006. The group sings various repertoire in various styles: from classical to pop and from opera to musical and everything in between. During concerts the members of Be Sharp sing a combination of solos, duets, trios and quartets, which are based around a theme.

The musical pieces are connected by use of acting and staging. This is done as mere transitions between pieces or as one continuous story. This is the strengths and uniqueness of Be Sharp. That doesn’t mean that Be Sharp doesn’t also perform only concertante style.

They have performed nationally as well as internationally with their various shows. To name a few: Mozart and more, Be Sharp has an annual Christmas show, which is held at the beautiful castle of Twickel in Delden.

Meet the members

Annette de Vries, soprano

Annete ound her love for singing at an early age, where she sang in a choir. Not very much later started singing lessons with the famous opera singer Evelien van der Linden. Soon after she interpreted different roles in opera and operetta. Annette is singing at Be Sharp from the start.

Linda Jacobs, alto

Linda sings since her childhood. She started out in the local musical and operetta company. Around the same time she started taking singing lessons from the famous singing teacher Anne Molenaar. Acting is also one of her passions. Linda enjoys combining the acting with the singing. This is what she likes about Be Sharp.
