About Us


The Dutch vocal group Be-Sharp is group of 4 singers, 2 men and 2 women, that was founded in 2006.
The group sings various repertoire in different styles: from classical to pop and from opera to musical and everything in between.

Our Music


During our 16 years of singing, Be-Sharp has build up quite a repertoire of music, ranging from classical to musical, to pop to jazz.
Also their style of show isn’t a standard or easy to explain style. You have to see to understand it.
