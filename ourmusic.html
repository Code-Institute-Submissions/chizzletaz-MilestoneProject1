People who have visited our shows sometimes have a hard time explaining what style our show has. Truth be told, we sometimes have a hard time explaining our shows to someone who has never seen our show. Our style isn’t described in one word. You could describe the different styles of music, the varying voicing, from solo to quartet. Or explain the storyline, which usually has many side stories. You could even describe the different styles of acting and still you haven’t explained the entire picture. Fortunately most of our shows have been recorded in part or as a whole show.
