Mozart and co

After their latest success of ‘Top Secret, the members of Be-Sharp now take you back to a fictional past. In their show “Mozart and co” they ask the question: ‘What if could go back in time and ask questions to composers like Mozart’. Their answers result in hilarious, strange, serious and sometimes sad situations. Music from many classical composers like Mozart, Vivaldi, Beethoven and Haydn. To counter the classical music, the protagonists react in the more modern musical languages like jazz, musical and even rap! Curious yet? Then get your tickets now!

20th Februari 2021 - 20.15h
Theater an der Wien - Vienna
